My Story

Built With Purpose

Hi, I'm Ange — an Exercise Physiologist based in Sydney. I graduated with a Bachelor of Exercise Physiology from the University of Sydney, and I'm passionate about helping people build strength, move better, and feel confident in their bodies.

My approach is science-backed and personalised — no cookie-cutter programs, just purposeful training built around you and your goals. I offer both online and face to face coaching through my program Built With Purpose, training out of Martin Place in Sydney's CBD.

My Why

"I want every woman to feel powerful in her body."

The fitness industry is full of noise — extreme diets, toxic before/afters, and promises that breed insecurity. I built FitWithAnge to be the opposite of that. A space where women are coached with respect, educated with evidence, and celebrated for every win — no matter how small.

How I Coach

My Coaching Philosophy

01

Strength First

Progressive overload is the foundation of every programme I write. Build muscle, boost metabolism, and feel genuinely capable.

02

Sustainable Over Extreme

Quick fixes don't last. Everything we do together is designed to become a lifestyle — not a 12-week sprint that ends in a rebound.

03

Education & Autonomy

I explain the why behind everything. My goal is for you to eventually not need me — because you understand your own body.

04

Whole-Person Approach

Sleep, stress, hormones, mindset — they all affect results. I look at the full picture, not just sets and reps.
