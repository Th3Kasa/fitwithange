Common questions

Before you reach out.

Are sessions online or in-person?
Both. Angelina offers face-to-face personal training at Anytime Fitness Martin Place in Sydney CBD and fully online coaching for clients anywhere in the world.
How quickly will Angelina reply?
Angelina replies to all enquiries within 24 hours on business days. For the fastest response, use the form above or send a DM on Instagram at @fitwith_ange.
Is there a trial session or free call?
Yes — a free 30-minute discovery call is available for all new clients. It’s a relaxed conversation to understand your goals, your history, and whether it’s the right fit. No commitment required.
