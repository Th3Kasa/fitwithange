A coach who actually
understands your body.

I'm Angelina — Exercise Physiologist and personal trainer based at Anytime Fitness Martin Place. I hold a Bachelor of Exercise Physiology from the University of Sydney, and I built FitWithAnge to close the gap between clinical knowledge and the kind of training that fits real life.

"I'm not interested in quick fixes. I want you to understand your body well enough to train confidently for the rest of your life."

— Angelina Farag, Exercise Physiologist

I work with women who are done second-guessing every rep and ready to train with real structure. That means evidence-based programs, honest check-ins, and zero cookie-cutter nonsense. Every phase is built with intent — and adjusted when life inevitably changes.

If you want to build strength that lasts — not just for a challenge, but for the long haul — I'd love to hear from you.
