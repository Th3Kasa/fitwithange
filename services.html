Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need any experience to work with you? +

Not at all. I work with complete beginners through to experienced lifters. Your programme is built around your current level and progresses at the right pace for you.

What equipment do I need for online coaching? +

It depends on your goals. I can programme for a commercial gym, a home gym with limited equipment, or even fully bodyweight. We'll discuss this on your free discovery call.

How quickly will I see results? +

Most clients notice strength improvements within 2–3 weeks. Visible body composition changes typically begin around 4–6 weeks with consistent training and nutrition. Results depend on your consistency, starting point, and goals.

Can I cancel or pause my coaching? +

Monthly coaching has no lock-in — you can cancel with 30 days notice. I also offer pause options for travel, illness, or life events. The 12-Week Programme is a fixed commitment but can be paused in genuine emergencies.
