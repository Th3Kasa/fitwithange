Questions

Frequently asked.

Do I need a gym membership?

For face-to-face PT, sessions are at Anytime Fitness Martin Place — you’ll need access. For online coaching, Angelina programmes around wherever you train: commercial gym, home setup, limited equipment, or fully bodyweight. Discuss it on your call.

How long until I see results?

Most clients notice strength improvements within 2–3 weeks. Visible body composition changes typically appear around 4–6 weeks with consistent training and nutrition. You’ll feel different long before you look different — and that shift matters.

Do you work with beginners?

Absolutely. Complete beginners through to experienced lifters. Your programme is built around your current level and progresses at the right pace for you — no assumption of prior experience, no embarrassment, no shortcuts.

Online vs face-to-face — how do I choose?

If you’re in Sydney CBD and want hands-on coaching energy, face-to-face is ideal. If you travel, work remotely, or want flexibility to train on your own schedule, online delivers the same quality of personalisation. The discovery call exists precisely to figure out which one fits your life.

Can I pause or cancel coaching?

Monthly coaching has no lock-in — cancel with 30 days’ notice. Pause options are available for travel, illness, or life events. The 12-Week Built With Purpose Programme is a fixed commitment, but can be paused in genuine emergencies.

Is nutrition guidance included?

All programmes include personalised nutrition guidance as a core component — not a bolt-on. Angelina is an accredited Exercise Physiologist, not a dietitian, so guidance is evidence-based and goal-focused rather than clinical treatment. If you need clinical dietary management she’ll refer you appropriately.
